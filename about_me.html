who am i?

I'm Grace! I'm a student at NYU Tisch's Interactive Media Arts. I'm also a part time designer at Boom Sports and have worked on apps such as Nascar FinishLine, NBC Sports Predictor and Play XFL. I focus on joyful, understandable and easily accesible design in my practice. To me, there is nothing worse than a confusing user experience. This belief may or may not stem from my inability to understand how TV remotes work. In my free time, I enjoy trying new foods and reading.
